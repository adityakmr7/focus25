Frequently Asked Questions

How do I customize my timer durations?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Timer Settings. You can adjust both Focus Duration and Break Duration using the time selectors. Changes apply to your next session.
What is the Pomodoro Technique?
The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that uses 25-minute focused work sessions followed by 5-minute breaks. After 4 sessions, you take a longer 15-30 minute break. This helps maintain concentration and prevents burnout.
How does flow state tracking work?
Flowzy monitors your session patterns, completion rates, and consistency to determine your flow intensity (Low, Medium, High). This helps you understand when you're most productive and how to optimize your focus sessions.
Can I use the app offline?
Yes! The timer, statistics, and most features work offline. Music tracks are cached for offline playback. Your data will sync when you reconnect to the internet.
How do notifications work?
Flowzy sends notifications when sessions complete, for break reminders, and goal achievements. You can customize notification preferences in Settings ‚Üí Notifications.
What happens if I close the app during a session?
Your session continues running in the background. You'll receive a notification when it's time for a break. The app automatically saves your progress.
How do I export my data?
In Settings ‚Üí Data & Sync, tap "Export Data" to download a complete backup of your goals, statistics, and settings in JSON format.
Can I sync data between devices?
Yes! Create an account and enable Cloud Sync in Settings. Your data will automatically synchronize across all devices where you're signed in.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Data & Sync ‚Üí Delete All Data. This will permanently remove your account and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.
What devices are supported?
Flowzy supports iOS 14+ and Android 8.0+. The app is optimized for phones and tablets with responsive design.

Troubleshooting

üîß App is crashing or freezing
  1. Force close and restart the app
  2. Restart your device
  3. Update to the latest app version
  4. Clear app cache (Android: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Flowzy ‚Üí Storage ‚Üí Clear Cache)
  5. If problems persist, contact support with your device model and OS version
üéµ Music not playing or downloading
  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Ensure you have sufficient storage space
  3. Try selecting a different track
  4. Restart the app
  5. Check if Silent Mode is enabled on your device
‚òÅÔ∏è Data not syncing
  1. Verify you're signed in to your account
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Manually trigger sync in Settings ‚Üí Data & Sync
  4. Sign out and sign back in
  5. Contact support if sync issues persist
üìä Statistics not updating
  1. Complete at least one full focus session
  2. Pull down to refresh the Statistics screen
  3. Check if the app has proper permissions
  4. Restart the app
  5. If using cloud sync, ensure you're connected to the internet
üîî Notifications not working
  1. Enable notifications in Settings ‚Üí Notifications
  2. Check device notification settings for Flowzy
  3. Ensure Do Not Disturb mode isn't blocking notifications
  4. Restart the app and grant notification permissions
  5. Check battery optimization settings (Android)

Advanced Features

üé® Customizing Themes
Access Settings ‚Üí Appearance ‚Üí Theme Customization to personalize colors, timer styles, and create custom themes. You can save multiple theme presets and switch between them.
üìà Understanding Analytics
Your Statistics screen shows:
  • Total Sessions: Completed focus sessions
  • Focus Time: Total time spent in productive focus
  • Focus Score: Efficiency rating based on interruptions
  • Current Streak: Consecutive days with completed sessions
  • Flow Intensity: Your current flow state level
üéØ Setting Smart Goals
Create SMART goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound):
  • Sessions Goal: Number of focus sessions per day/week
  • Focus Time Goal: Total productive hours
  • Streak Goal: Consecutive days of activity
  • Consistency Goal: Percentage of days with completed sessions
